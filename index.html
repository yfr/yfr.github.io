My talk about webcomponents

This is just a prove of concept for my talk about webcomponents. I created a component called `yfr-slide` and used it to create the presentation.

You can use the `right` (next slide) and `down` (next step in current slide) buttons to navigate the slides forward. But there is no back functionality.
